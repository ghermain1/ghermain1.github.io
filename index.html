GHERMAIN

Ghermain is a Ukrainian-born artist currently living and working in Brooklyn, New York. With a degree in graphic design, he has spent the past decade immersed in the vanguard movements of visual art, engaging in both commercial projects and exhibiting in multiple personal and group shows.

As a multidisciplinary artist, Ghermain explores the intersections of muralism, graphic design, fashion, and literature, infusing each project with his unique vision and passion.

For inquiries about commissions or to ask questions, please feel free to reach out via email. If you would like to support my work, consider following the Patreon link.

Thank you for your interest!
