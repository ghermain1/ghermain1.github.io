GHERMAIN

Ghermain is a Ukrainian-born artist currently living and working in Brooklyn, New York. With over a decade in the contemporary art scene, he has participated in variety of projects and showcased his work in solo and group exhibitions.

As a multidisciplinary creator, Ghermain explores the intersections of muralism, oil painting, graphic design, fashion, video and literature, resulting in a vibrant mix of cultures and influences.

For inquiries about commissions or artistic questions, feel free to reach out via email. If you'd like to support his creative journey, check out the Patreon link.

Thank you for your interest!
